A Legacy of Excellence

Since 1892, our atelier has been the trusted choice of society's most distinguished families. Four generations of master tailors have refined our craft, creating garments that transcend fashion to become treasured heirlooms.

Our commitment to traditional techniques, combined with the finest materials sourced from renowned mills across Europe, ensures that every piece meets the exacting standards of old-world craftsmanship.
